Here are the standard Bootstrap breakpoints: xs (Extra Small): Used for screens smaller than 576px in width (e.g., smartphones). sm (Small): Used for screens between 576px and 768px in width (e.g., small tablets). md (Medium): Used for screens between 768px and 992px in width (e.g., larger tablets and small desktops). lg (Large): Used for screens between 992px and 1200px in width (e.g., larger desktops). xl (Extra Large): Used for screens larger than 1200px in width (e.g., very large desktops).
